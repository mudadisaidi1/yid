About YID

Youth Innovation for Development (YID) is a youth-led, non-profit organization empowering young people through innovation, digital skills training, entrepreneurship, and environmental action. We serve youth in refugee and host communities to build self-reliant and resilient futures.

Our Programs

Digital Skills & ICT Training

We provide computer literacy, coding, graphic design, and online entrepreneurship skills to bridge the digital divide.

Education & Skills Development

YID supports education access, mentorship, and vocational skills training for youth empowerment.

Women & Girls Empowerment

We empower women and girls through vocational training and entrepreneurship programs.

Environmental & Climate Action

Our youth lead tree planting, recycling initiatives, and climate awareness campaigns.

Community & Peacebuilding

We promote teamwork, leadership, and social cohesion through youth-led initiatives.

Our Impact

  • Training youth in digital and vocational skills
  • Empowering women through entrepreneurship
  • Promoting environmental conservation
  • Supporting youth leadership & innovation
  • Strengthening community resilience
